About Us

Cruzin’ was formed by a small group of friends brought together by a shared love of food and the stories behind it. Today, we gather recipes through our own journeys and research, while also welcoming submissions from home cooks, chefs, and storytellers everywhere.

Our goal is to create a space where flavors, traditions, and voices from across the globe can be shared, explored, and remembered—one recipe at a time.
